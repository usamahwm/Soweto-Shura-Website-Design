Who We Are

About Soweto Muslim
Shura Council

A non-profit community organisation dedicated to advancing Islam and fostering unity, development, and spiritual growth in the Muslim community of Soweto since 2009.

Our Story

Born from a
Community Call

Soweto Muslim Shura was formulated in 2009 — a direct response to a call made by the late Imaam Ibrahim, who expressed deep concern about the decline of deeni activities within the Muslim community of Soweto.

From that call, a movement was born. Our aim is to advance Islam in Soweto, engage meaningfully in community development, and create a shared vision that unites the Muslim community in purpose and action.

Over 18 years of service, we have grown from a concerned gathering into a structured council with dedicated leadership, active programmes, and a community of thousands behind us.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

Everything we do is grounded in three pillars that guide our work, our relationships, and our service to the community.

🎯

Our Mission

To foster a strong and vibrant Muslim community in Soweto by providing spiritual guidance, social support, and educational opportunities — helping our members thrive in faith and daily life.

🌱

Our Vision

To establish a united Muslim community capable of sustainable change and growth — a community that is self-sufficient, spiritually grounded, and actively contributing to the township.

⚖️

Our Values

Unity, integrity, and compassion — we strive to promote understanding and service within our community and beyond, guided at all times by Islamic principles of justice and care.
