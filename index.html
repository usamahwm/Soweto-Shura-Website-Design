The Programme

The Soweto Family
Support Initiative

A flagship programme aimed at strengthening Muslim families — beginning with community consultations and two landmark events, creating a safe space for families to address challenges together.

Our Goal

Build community buy-in and move forward together, with support from the Islamic Family Court, Independent Islamic Court, and Islamic Careline.

Why Us

A Range of Community Services

Comprehensive support for Muslim families in Soweto — from marriage guidance to estate planning, all grounded in Islamic principles.

💍

Pre-Marital Classes

Guidance for couples preparing for marriage, building strong foundations in faith and partnership.

🤝

Marriage Counselling

Ongoing support for married couples navigating challenges with compassion and Islamic guidance.

⚖️

Mediation & Arbitration

Fair and dignified dispute resolution guided by Islamic principles of justice and mercy.

📜

Talaqs & Annulments

Sensitive support throughout the process of ending a marriage with care and Islamic guidance.

🏠

Estate Planning

Managing assets and legacy in line with Islamic inheritance law for your family's future.

❤️

Post-Marital Welfare

Long-term social welfare and community resources for individuals and families in need.
